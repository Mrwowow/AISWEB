Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dietetic services

Dietitians are regulated healthcare professionals registered with the HCPC. The title "dietitian" is protected by law, ensuring you receive advice from a qualified professional. Nutritionists may not have the same level of training or regulation.

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes, allowing time for a comprehensive assessment. Follow-up appointments are usually 30 minutes to review progress and make adjustments.
